\section{Bring Your Own Device}

Beginning with academic year 2027-28, undergraduate and graduate students enrolled in any major or minor offered by the Department of Computer Science are required to own and use a computer device that meets the specifications of this policy.

If you enroll in a Computer Science course, without being a CS major or minor, you are strongly encouraged to comply with this policy. The following courses are build around the assumption that every student in the classroom has a qualified device as defined in this policy: COMP 125, COMP 141, COMP 150, COMP 170, COMP 264, COMP 271, COMP 272, and COMP 363.
